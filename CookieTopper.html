Cookie Topper

This project was completed by my classmate, Sean Healy, and I in the Project Lead the Way class Principles of Engineering. In this assignment we were tasked with topping a Chips Ahoy cookie with whip cream from a can. Supplied to us was a VEX Robotics kit and ROBOTC program. We started the process off with a few design sketches. All went well with the brainstorming process and we had a set plan. During the building process we had a slight setback trying to figure out how to actuate the top of the whip cream can. After coming up with a rack and pinion method to move the tip of the nozzle we started the final stage of construction. As shown in the video, motors move the conveyor belt which rotates under a color sensor. We ended up using a color sensor after trying to use a distance center due to the bounciness of the conveyor belt. This meant for calibration of the sensor to the color of the cookies.
