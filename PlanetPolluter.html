Planet Polluter



This project was completed by Ryan Lung, Brock Fenbert, Blake Mchale, Josh Field, and myself as part of the 2018 NASA Space Apps Hackathon hosted by Binnovative. The idea of Planet Polluter was inspired by Plague Inc. a game where you try and infect the entire world. In our version, the player attempts to pollute the entire world. In order to win, one has to reach the CO2 levels that are present in 2018 before the time reaches 2018.




The game incorporates data from NASA's website and compares your current level of carbon dioxide throughout the game with the actual data that was present in the atmosphere that year. Our goal for the game was to raise awareness about the effects of carbon pollution and some of the major causes.
***Note: Game is a very basic prototype***

Our real goal at the end of the day was to learn Unity and C# which we did build some foundations in.
